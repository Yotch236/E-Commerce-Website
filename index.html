About Us

Fresh Moto Vending Machine is a self-service device designed to clean, sanitize, and dry helmets efficiently. It utilizes advanced technologies like UV-C sanitization to eliminate 99.9% of germs, bacteria, and viruses. Fully automated and requiring no manpower, these machines are often coin-operated or support mobile payments. They offer a quick and convenient solution for riders to maintain helmet hygiene, ensuring safety and comfort on every ride.
