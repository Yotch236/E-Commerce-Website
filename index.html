About Us

Fresh Moto Vending Machine is a self-service device designed to clean, sanitize, and dry helmets efficiently. It utilizes advanced technologies like UV-C sanitization to eliminate 99.9% of germs, bacteria, and viruses. Fully automated and requiring no manpower, these machines are often coin-operated or support mobile payments. They offer a quick and convenient solution for riders to maintain helmet hygiene, ensuring safety and comfort on every ride.

Vision

We envision being the best in automated helmet cleaning service, a name synonymous with reliability, innovation, and environmental consciousness. We see the growing of Moto Fresh Vending Machine Service into schools, transport terminals, business hubs, and urban communities throughout the nation-wide. We would like to continuously improve the technology and services while setting a national benchmark for helmet hygiene and promoting responsible waste management and sustainable living. Mission

Moto Fresh Vending Machine Service is committed to providing fast, efficient, high-quality sanitation of motorcycle helmets via modern, self-service technologies. Our mission includes ensuring rider safety and personal hygiene through accessible, affordable, and environmentally sound means of cleaning helmets. We intend to fulfill the needs of motorcycle riders, especially students and commuters, by saving time while ensuring the cleanliness, freshness, and safety of their helmets. We intend to ensure a cleaner, healthier, sustainable, and more customer-friendly experience for riders through innovation, customer-focused service, and sustainable practices.
